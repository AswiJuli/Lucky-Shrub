Our Story

Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Euphorbia

Euphorbia is a diverse genus of flowering plants, commonly known as spurge. They are known for their unique and often striking appearance, with some species resembling cacti. Euphorbias are popular in gardens and as houseplants due to their low maintenance and drought tolerance.

Croton

Croton is a genus of flowering plants in the family Euphorbiaceae, native to tropical regions of the Americas and the Pacific. They are known for their vibrant, colorful leaves and are popular as ornamental plants in gardens and as houseplants.
